The parties agree as follows:

1. Purpose and Appointment
1.1 The Principal appoints the EU Representative, and the EU Representative accepts appointment, as the Principal's representative in the European Union for purposes of Article 27 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).  
1.2 This appointment covers the processing activities of Rollerstat in scope of GDPR Article 3(2).

2. Scope of Representative Services
2.1 The EU Representative shall serve as a contact point for:
- supervisory authorities in the EU/EEA; and
- data subjects located in the EU/EEA,
in matters related to processing under this mandate.

2.2 The EU Representative shall:
- receive GDPR-related communications addressed to the Principal;
- forward them to the Principal promptly under Section 3;
- maintain accurate contact details and availability for contact.

2.3 The EU Representative is not responsible for determining purposes/means of processing and is not a controller by virtue of this appointment alone.

3. Forwarding and Response Workflow
3.1 The EU Representative must forward all GDPR-related regulator/data subject requests to the Principal without undue delay and in any event:
- within 1 business day for supervisory authority communications; and
- within 2 business days for data subject requests.

3.2 Forwarding shall include all available details and copies of attachments/metadata needed for Principal response handling.

3.3 If a request appears urgent (deadline, enforcement risk, or complaint escalation), the EU Representative will flag it as URGENT in subject line and notify by secondary channel (if provided).

4. Principal Obligations
4.1 The Principal remains fully responsible for GDPR compliance and all decisions on responses.  
4.2 The Principal will provide the EU Representative with:
- up-to-date processing summaries;
- current privacy policy URL and legal contact details;
- point-of-contact instructions for urgent matters.

5. Confidentiality and Security
5.1 Each party shall keep confidential all non-public information exchanged under this Agreement.  
5.2 The EU Representative shall apply reasonable organizational and technical safeguards to protect personal data and legal correspondence.

6. Records and Evidence
6.1 The EU Representative shall keep records of inbound GDPR communications and timestamps of forwarding for auditability.  
6.2 Upon request, the EU Representative shall provide forwarding logs related to this mandate.
