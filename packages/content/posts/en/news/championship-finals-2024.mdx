# European Roller Hockey Championship Finals 2024

The European Roller Hockey Championship came to a spectacular conclusion this weekend as Spain secured a dramatic victory over Portugal in the final match, winning 4-3 in overtime.

## Match Highlights

The final was a showcase of the highest level of roller hockey, with both teams demonstrating exceptional skill and determination. Spain's captain, Carlos Mendez, scored the winning goal in the 78th minute of overtime, sending the Spanish fans into ecstasy.

### Key Moments

- **First Half**: Portugal took an early 2-0 lead with goals from Silva and Costa
- **Second Half**: Spain fought back with three consecutive goals from Rodriguez, Mendez, and Garcia
- **Overtime**: Mendez's decisive goal secured Spain's 15th European Championship title

## Tournament Statistics

This year's championship saw record attendance numbers and was broadcast to over 50 countries worldwide. The tournament featured 16 teams competing over two weeks of intense matches.

The victory marks Spain's return to the top of European roller hockey after a three-year drought, while Portugal's strong performance throughout the tournament has positioned them as serious contenders for future competitions.

## Looking Ahead

With the European Championship concluded, attention now turns to the World Championships scheduled for later this year in Argentina, where both Spain and Portugal will be among the favorites to compete for the ultimate prize in roller hockey.
